# Venture Capital and Fundraising

How venture works from both sides of the table — term sheets, incentives, and raising money well.

3 item(s), ranked by signal. ⭐ = core / must-experience.

---

### ⭐ [How to Raise Money](../cards/pg-how-to-raise-money.md)
*Paul Graham · 2013 · Essay · Intermediate*

A detailed Paul Graham essay on the mechanics and psychology of raising a seed round: getting to a strong position first, talking to investors in parallel, managing momentum, and not letting fundraising consume the company.

**Why it matters:** One of the clearest practical guides to seed fundraising from someone who's seen thousands of rounds.

### [Secrets of Sand Hill Road](../cards/secrets-of-sand-hill-road.md)
*Scott Kupor · 2019 · Book · Intermediate*

A clear insider explanation of how venture capital actually works — fund economics, term sheets, board dynamics, and what VCs are optimizing for — written by a managing partner at Andreessen Horowitz.

**Why it matters:** The most accessible explanation of the VC machine from the inside.

### [Venture Deals](../cards/venture-deals.md)
*Brad Feld, Jason Mendelson · 2011 · Book · Intermediate*

A practical, founder-friendly guide to understanding and negotiating venture financings term by term, written by experienced VCs. The standard reference for what's in a term sheet and why.

**Why it matters:** The go-to handbook for founders who want to understand deals before signing them.
