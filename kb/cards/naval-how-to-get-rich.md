## Summary
An expanded podcast/essay series on building wealth through ownership and leverage — code, media, capital, and labor — plus the role of specific knowledge, accountability, and long-term games with long-term people.

## Key takeaways

- Modern leverage is code and media — permissionless and infinitely scalable.
