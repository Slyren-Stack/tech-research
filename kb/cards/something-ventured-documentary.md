## Summary
A documentary on the birth of the venture capital industry, following the early VCs who backed Apple, Intel, Genentech, Atari, and Cisco, and how their bets created Silicon Valley.

## Key takeaways
- Shows how a handful of early investors catalyzed entire industries.
- Illuminates the founder–investor relationship at its best.
- Grounds modern VC in its surprisingly recent origins.

## Why it matters
