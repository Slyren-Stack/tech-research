## Summary
The paper behind AlexNet, the deep convolutional network that crushed the ImageNet 2012 competition and kicked off the modern deep learning era by showing GPUs + big data + deep nets work at scale.

## Key takeaways
- Demonstrated that deep CNNs trained on GPUs dramatically outperform prior computer vision methods.
- Popularized techniques like ReLU activations and dropout for regularization.
- Marked the inflection point where deep learning became the dominant paradigm.

## Why it matters
