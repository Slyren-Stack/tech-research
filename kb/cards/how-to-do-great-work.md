# How to Do Great Work

**Paul Graham** · 2023 · Essay
**Category:** Founder Mental Models → Career & Craft
**Difficulty:** Beginner · **Status:** `core` · **Credibility:** 9/10 · **Usefulness:** 9/10

## Summary
A long essay distilling what people who do great work tend to have in common: working on things you're genuinely curious about, the discipline to follow that curiosity, and the courage to work on ambitious problems.

## Key takeaways
- Great work usually comes from deep curiosity, not forced ambition.
- Choose problems at the intersection of what you're interested in and what's important.
- Consistency, taste, and the willingness to look stupid compound over time.
