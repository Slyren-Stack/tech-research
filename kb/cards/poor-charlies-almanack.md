## Summary
A collection of Charlie Munger's speeches and wisdom, centered on the idea of a 'latticework of mental models' drawn from many disciplines to make better decisions and avoid predictable folly.

## Key takeaways
- Build a latticework of mental models across disciplines, not siloed expertise.
- Invert problems: study how to fail, then avoid it.
- Understand human misjudgment and incentives to think more clearly.

## Why it matters
