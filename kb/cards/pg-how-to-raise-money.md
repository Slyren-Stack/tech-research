# How to Raise Money

**Paul Graham** · 2013 · Essay

## Summary
A detailed Paul Graham essay on the mechanics and psychology of raising a seed round: getting to a strong position first, talking to investors in parallel, managing momentum, and not letting fundraising consume the company.

## Key takeaways
- Raise from a position of strength; be default-alive, not desperate.
- Pursue investors in parallel to create real momentum.
- Fundraising is a distraction — get it done and get back to building.

## Why it matters
One of the clearest practical guides to seed fundraising from someone who's seen thousands of rounds.
