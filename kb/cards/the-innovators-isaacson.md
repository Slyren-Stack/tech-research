## Summary
A sweeping history of the digital revolution from Ada Lovelace to the web, emphasizing that innovation is collaborative — a relay of teams and ideas rather than lone geniuses.

## Key takeaways
- Digital innovation was driven by collaboration, not solitary genius.
- Connects a long lineage from Babbage and Lovelace to modern computing.
- A broad, accessible map of how the digital age was built.
