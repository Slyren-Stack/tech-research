# Attention Is All You Need

**Ashish Vaswani et al. (Google)** · 2017 · Paper
**Category:** AI Foundations → Deep Learning Architectures
**Difficulty:** Advanced · **Status:** `core` · **Credibility:** 10/10 · **Usefulness:** 9/10

## Summary
The paper that introduced the Transformer, an architecture built entirely on attention mechanisms with no recurrence or convolution. It became the foundation for virtually every modern large language model.

## Key takeaways
- Self-attention lets a model weigh relationships between all tokens in a sequence in parallel.
- Removing recurrence made training dramatically more parallelizable and scalable.
- The architecture generalized far beyond translation into the backbone of GPT, BERT, and modern LLMs.

## Why it matters
Almost everything in the current AI wave traces back to this architecture. Reading it is the single best way to understand what an LLM actually is.
