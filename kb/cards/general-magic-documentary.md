# General Magic

**Sarah Kerruish, Matt Maude** · 2018 · Documentary

## Summary
A documentary about General Magic, the 1990s Apple spin-off that tried to build the smartphone decades too early. It failed commercially but its alumni went on to shape the iPhone, Android, eBay, and more.

## Key takeaways
- Vision can be right while timing is fatally wrong.
- 'Failed' companies can seed an entire industry through their people.
- A moving look at belief, ambition, and the cost of being early.

## Why it matters
A rare, honest film about a brilliant failure that quietly shaped modern tech.

## Audience fit
Founders, product people, anyone who's bet on a big vision.
