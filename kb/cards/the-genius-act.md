# The GENIUS Act

**U.S. Congress** · 2025 · Legislation
**Category:** Crypto and Web3 → Regulation
**Difficulty:** Intermediate · **Status:** `reviewed` · **Credibility:** 8/10 · **Usefulness:** 7/10

## Summary
The first major US federal crypto law, creating a framework for dollar-backed stablecoins with 1:1 reserves, audits, and disclosures.

## 💬 Good conversation topic
The pivot point for 'is crypto finally legitimate' debates with institutions.

## Key takeaways
- First comprehensive US stablecoin framework
- Requires 1:1 reserves and regular audits
- Clarifies compliant stablecoins are neither securities nor commodities

## Why it matters
Moved US crypto from regulatory limbo to law; global crypto briefly topped $4T after enactment.
